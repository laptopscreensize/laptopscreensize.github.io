Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

My Screen Size ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that our screen size detection tool operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). The screen resolution, device type, pixel density, and other display metrics are calculated locally on your device using JavaScript browser APIs. These values are never transmitted to any server, database, or third party.

Your visit is completely anonymous. We do not use analytics scripts, tracking pixels, fingerprinting techniques, or session recording tools.

2. No Cookies

This website does not set any cookies, local storage entries, or any other persistent identifiers. No consent banners are required because no tracking occurs.

3. Third-Party Services

Our tool does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media plugins. The only external resources loaded are Google Fonts (for typography) which may record standard HTTP request data (like IP address) as described in Google's Privacy Policy. However, we do not control or have access to that data. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy for font services.

We do not use any CDN that tracks users. All detection logic is self-contained in static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files hosted on GitHub Pages (or your hosting provider). The underlying hosting provider may collect standard server logs (IP address, date/time, requested pages) for security and performance purposes. Those logs are not accessible or used by us for user tracking.

4. User-Provided Information

The optional "Physical Size Estimator" allows you to manually enter your screen diagonal (in inches). This input stays inside your browser and is never transmitted. We do not store or access that value after you leave the page.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no data collection occurs, COPPA compliance is inherent.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect clarifications or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of any revised policy.
