Process

How It Works

Zero servers. Zero tracking. All detection runs locally in your browser.

01

Browser API Access

We query the browser's native screen, window, and navigator APIs — no permissions needed, no data sent anywhere.

02

Real-time Calculation

Aspect ratios, pixel density, physical dimensions, and breakpoints are calculated on the fly using mathematical formulas.

03

Live Updates

Resize your window, rotate your device, or change resolution — all values update instantly via event listeners.

04

Private by Design

Everything stays on your device. No analytics, no fingerprinting, no cookies. Your data never leaves your browser.
